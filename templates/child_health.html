Newborn Babies

Signs and Symptoms

  • Jaundice (yellowing of the skin and eyes)
  • Sugarcane-like breath (sweet-smelling breath)
  • Swollen abdomen
  • Poor feeding
  • Vomiting
  • Dark urine
  • Pale stools
  • Excessive sleepiness
  • Irritability

Treatments and Procedures

  • Phototherapy for jaundice
  • Medications to improve liver function
  • Surgical procedures if necessary (e.g., Kasai procedure for biliary atresia)
  • Intravenous fluids and nutrition
  • Monitoring and supportive care

Infants

Signs and Symptoms

  • Jaundice (yellowing of the skin and eyes)
  • Sugarcane-like breath (sweet-smelling breath)
  • Swollen abdomen
  • Poor weight gain
  • Vomiting
  • Dark urine
  • Pale stools
  • Excessive sleepiness
  • Irritability
  • Failure to thrive

Treatments and Procedures

  • Phototherapy for jaundice
  • Medications to improve liver function
  • Surgical procedures if necessary (e.g., Kasai procedure for biliary atresia)
  • Intravenous fluids and nutrition
  • Monitoring and supportive care
  • Dietary modifications

Toddlers

Signs and Symptoms

  • Jaundice (yellowing of the skin and eyes)
  • Sugarcane-like breath (sweet-smelling breath)
  • Swollen abdomen
  • Poor appetite
  • Vomiting
  • Dark urine
  • Pale stools
  • Fatigue
  • Irritability
  • Delayed growth and development

Treatments and Procedures

  • Phototherapy for jaundice
  • Medications to improve liver function
  • Surgical procedures if necessary (e.g., liver transplant for severe cases)
  • Intravenous fluids and nutrition
  • Monitoring and supportive care
  • Dietary modifications
  • Physical therapy and developmental support
